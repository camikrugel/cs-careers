# Data Schema Documentation

This document describes the structure and contents of all 8 CSV output files generated by the Reddit career data processing pipeline.

---

## 1. Topic Analysis (topic_analysis.csv)

Purpose: Distribution of posts across topic categories

Columns:
- topic (string): Topic category
- post_count (integer): Number of posts in category
- percentage (float): Percentage of total posts

Topic Categories: internship, interview, salary, job_search, resume, projects, courses, new_grad, faang

Sample Row:
topic,post_count,percentage
salary,1245,18.5

---

## 2. Sentiment by Topic (sentiment_by_topic.csv)

Purpose: Sentiment analysis aggregated by topic

Columns:
- topic (string): Topic category
- sentiment (string): Sentiment classification (positive, neutral, negative)
- post_count (integer): Number of posts with this sentiment
- percentage (float): Percentage within topic

Sentiment Categories:
- positive (polarity > 0.1)
- neutral (-0.1 <= polarity <= 0.1)
- negative (polarity < -0.1)

Sample Row:
topic,sentiment,post_count,percentage
salary,positive,450,36.1

---

## 3. Posts by Industry (posts_by_industry.csv)

Purpose: Post distribution across career fields/industries

Columns:
- industry (string): Career field/industry
- post_count (integer): Number of posts mentioning this industry
- percentage (float): Percentage of total posts

Industry Categories: FAANG, Startups, BigTech, Finance, Healthcare, Government, Education, Other

Sample Row:
industry,post_count,percentage
FAANG,890,13.2

---

## 4. Salary Statistics (salary_stats.csv)

Purpose: Compensation analysis by industry

Columns:
- industry (string): Career field
- avg_salary (float): Average salary mentioned
- min_salary (float): Minimum salary mentioned
- max_salary (float): Maximum salary mentioned
- salary_mention_posts (integer): Number of posts with salary data
- salary_mention_percentage (float): % of posts mentioning salary

Notes:
- Salary range: 30k-500k (outliers excluded)
- Salary patterns: $120k, $120,000, "tc: 120k"
- All values in USD

Sample Row:
industry,avg_salary,min_salary,max_salary,salary_mention_posts,salary_mention_percentage
FAANG,145000.0,60000.0,500000.0,245,27.5

---

## 5. Experience Distribution (experience_distribution.csv)

Purpose: Career stage breakdown

Columns:
- experience_level (string): Career stage
- post_count (integer): Number of posts
- percentage (float): Percentage of total

Experience Levels:
- intern (internship, co-op)
- entry (0-2 years, entry-level, junior, grad)
- mid (2-5 years, mid-level)
- senior (5-10 years, senior, principal)
- exec (10+ years, manager, director, CTO, VP)

Sample Row:
experience_level,post_count,percentage
entry,1200,17.9

---

## 6. Skills Summary (skills_summary.csv)

Purpose: Ranked list of most mentioned technologies/skills

Columns:
- skill (string): Technology/language/framework
- skill_count (integer): Number of posts mentioning skill
- percentage (float): Percentage of total posts
- rank (integer): Ranking (1 = most mentioned)

Skill Categories:
- Languages: Python, Java, JavaScript, C++, Go, Rust, C#, TypeScript, PHP, Ruby
- Frontend: React, Vue, Angular, Next.js, Svelte
- Backend: Django, Spring, FastAPI, Express, Rails
- Cloud: AWS, GCP, Azure
- DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, Jenkins, CI/CD
- Databases: SQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Redis

Sample Row:
skill,skill_count,percentage,rank
Python,1450,21.6,1

---

## 7. Temporal Trends (temporal_trends.csv)

Purpose: Post volume and sentiment over time

Columns:
- year_month (string): Month in YYYY-MM format
- sentiment (string): Sentiment category (positive, neutral, negative)
- post_count (integer): Number of posts
- percentage (float): Percentage of posts that month

Uses:
- Identify seasonal trends in job market
- Track sentiment changes over time
- Detect significant events affecting discussions

Sample Row:
year_month,sentiment,post_count,percentage
2023-01,positive,450,33.3

---

## 8. Network Metrics (network_metrics.csv)

Purpose: High-level aggregate statistics and text metrics

Columns:
- metric (string): Metric name
- value (float): Metric value

Metrics Included:
- total_posts: Total Reddit posts processed
- avg_post_length: Average length of post body (words)
- avg_title_length: Average length of post title (words)
- posts_with_salary: Posts mentioning salary
- posts_with_skills: Posts mentioning technologies
- avg_engagement_score: Average post engagement
- unique_skills_mentioned: Distinct skills/technologies found
- unique_industries: Distinct industries mentioned

Sample Row:
metric,value
total_posts,6721.0

---

## Data Types & Encoding

All CSV Files:
- Encoding: UTF-8
- Delimiter: Comma (,)
- Quote Character: Double quote (")
- Line Terminator: LF

Column Types:
- string: Text (no quotes unless needed)
- integer: Whole numbers (no decimals)
- float: Decimal numbers (double precision)

## Data Quality Notes

Validation Applied:
- Salary range: 30k-500k (outliers removed)
- Post count > 0 (empty posts excluded)
- Text cleaned (URLs, markdown removed)
- Duplicates filtered

Potential Limitations:
- Sentiment based on polarity heuristic (not ML model)
- Industry detection via regex patterns
- Skill extraction matches keyword list (50+ items)
